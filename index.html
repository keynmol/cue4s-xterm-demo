Cue4s ❤️ Xterm.js ❤️ Scala.js

Demonstration of cue4s - cross-platform Scala 3 library for CLI prompts that works on JS, Native, and the JVM. This demonstration is performed by running the Scala.js version of the library in terminals emulated by Xterm.js. Click on any of the terminals and start typing!
